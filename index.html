About

A trio exploring the vastness of guitar music (and other pluckable instruments) from around the world, 'Pluck!' is a musical adventure spanning Malian, flamenco, Brazilian, Japanese, Latin American, bluegrass, folk, gypsy-jazz, early-electric-guitar, Celtic, and many other styles of music. Join them as their armada of guitars pull into port for a night and let them regale you with the sounds they have collected on their journeys. Their repertoire consists of both traditional and modern music, as well as original music inspired by the styles they perform. Each player has their own take on plucked string music and they fuse their styles under a love of playfulness, improvisation, excellence, and a well-placed music dad joke.
